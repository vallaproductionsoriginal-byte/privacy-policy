Privacy Policy for Harmony Engine Plus

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Harmony Engine Plus is an Android MIDI utility app developed by Valla Productions.

Data Collection

Harmony Engine Plus does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit personal data.

Internet Access

Harmony Engine Plus does not require an internet connection and does not send user data to external servers.

MIDI Functionality

The app uses Android MIDI features only to send MIDI notes, chords, scales, and arpeggios to compatible MIDI devices, DAWs, or external hardware selected by the user.

Analytics and Advertising

Harmony Engine Plus does not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection services.

Permissions

Harmony Engine Plus only uses permissions required for its MIDI functionality, if applicable. No personal or sensitive user data is collected through these permissions.

Children's Privacy

Harmony Engine Plus is not specifically directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app functionality changes. Any updates will be published on this page.
